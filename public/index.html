SkillsUSA at Reading Muhlenberg Career & Technology Center

At RMCTC, SkillsUSA is more than just an organization; it's a cornerstone of our educational approach. As a chapter with students from all program areas, we embody the spirit of SkillsUSA through our commitment to personal development, workplace skills, and technical excellence.

Through the Framework, SkillsUSA students hone their hands-on skills against current industry standards in more than 130 occupational areas, from 3-D Animation to Welding and nearly everything in between. At the same time, they develop the transformative career-readiness skills — teamwork, communication, professionalism, leadership and more — that fuel career and life success.

Our Approach

We integrate the SkillsUSA framework into every aspect of our educational experience. Whether it's through classroom learning, co-op opportunities, internships, or community service, we strive to equip our students with the skills they need to succeed in their careers from day one.

Community Engagement

Our commitment to SkillsUSA extends beyond the classroom. Through partnerships with local businesses like East Penn Manufacturing, Fraser Advanced, and Reading Hospital, among others, our students gain real-world experience while making meaningful contributions to our community.

Personal and Professional Growth

At RMCTC, we prioritize the development of essential skills such as resume writing, interviewing, and conflict resolution, all of which are integral parts of the SkillsUSA framework. Additionally, our technical skills curriculum is seamlessly integrated into our daily schedule, ensuring that every student receives a comprehensive education.

Core Values

We are guided by the core values of integrity, respect, responsibility, community, and service. These values inform everything we do, from our interactions with students and staff to our partnerships with industry leaders.

Impact

The impact of SkillsUSA at RMCTC is evident in the success stories of our students. From gaining confidence and discovering passions to securing employment in high-demand fields, SkillsUSA has transformed countless lives within our school community.

Symbols and Traditions

From the iconic SkillsUSA emblem to the solemn recitation of the SkillsUSA Pledge, our school embraces the symbols and traditions that define the SkillsUSA experience. These rituals serve as reminders of our shared commitment to professionalism, service, and lifelong learning.


Join Us

At RMCTC, SkillsUSA isn't just an organization; it's a community dedicated to empowering students and shaping the future of the skilled trades. Join us as we continue to uphold the ideals of SkillsUSA and prepare the next generation of skilled professionals and leaders.
